Top 10 Best Institutes for NEET in India

  • June 10, 2022June 22, 2022

The National Eligibility-cum-Entrance Test (NEET) is an all-India common medical entrance exam conducted by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). It serves as the sole criterion to take admission into various MBBS and BDS courses in Indias government and private medical colleges. The NEET exam replaced the All India Pre Medical Test (AIPMT) and other state-level entrance exams to make the admission process more fair and transparent, with the goal of eventually eliminating confusion around multiple entrance exams.

1) Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology Allahabad

The Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology is a public, national engineering institute located at Allahabad in Uttar Pradesh. The Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology was established on June 14, 1958 and it was named after Pandit Motilal Nehru, an eminent Indian nationalist leader who founded Jamia Millia Islamia. The institute is affiliated to Uttar Pradesh Technical University (UPTU) and approved by AICTE and NITA. Apart from its undergraduate programs, MNIT has postgraduate programs like M.Tech., MBA, MCA and PhD. It offers regular courses under semester system as well as distance learning courses (online). Recently a new Department of Biosciences & Bioengineering has been introduced in MNIT.

2) Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University

Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University is located in Maharashtra. It is an autonomous public university established by Government of Maharashtra. The institute was founded on 16th May-1973. Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University was formerly known as Central Agricultural University (CAU). This institute is one of a pioneer institution that offers under graduate programs to Bachelors and Post Graduate Programs to Masters and doctorate level courses. This institution also organizes various undergraduate and postgraduate courses like BA, BSc, BEd, MA, MEd, MBA and Ph.D degree programs.

3) Sri Venkateswara University

Established way back in 1954, SVU is one of Indias leading centers of higher education. It has seven campuses located across Andhra Pradesh, Telangana and Tamil Nadu, with a staff strength of more than 3,000. In fact, it offers courses in around 25 disciplines including engineering and technology; law; arts; and medicine and health sciences among others. The university has a rich history behind it: For example, its campus at Tirupati was designed by internationally renowned architect Le Corbusier.

4) Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Anantapur

This university has done exceptionally well when it comes to producing successful candidates who are appearing for various entrance exams. According to a report by TOI, more than 70% of its students have successfully made it to medical colleges in Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh and Kerala. The infrastructure of JNTU Anantapur is quite good with huge spaces that are meant for outdoor sports. It was ranked as one of Indias best colleges by Business Today and had featured on QS Top Universities list from 2016-2018. It also featured among Indias top 20 research institutions back in 2013 and is currently working on an iGEM project under an agreement with Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT).

5) Anna University Chennai

Anna University of Technology, formerly known as Anna University is a public state university. It is located at Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. It was formed by merging four older universities, namely Bharathidasan University, Theagaraya College of Engineering, Madras Institute of Technology and College of Engineering Guindy. The university was formed on 4 September 2007 and started functioning from 3 October 2007 with 36 colleges (22 under Anna University and 14 under its autonomous colleges) from around Chennai city and nearby districts. When it started functioning there were 22 schools which offered undergraduate courses while 13 provided postgraduate courses in various streams under five faculties: engineering & technology; sciences; architecture & planning; humanities & social sciences; business administration.

6) Dr. B.R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology Jalandhar

The Dr. B.R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology is one of Indias leading engineering colleges, with its Jalandhar campus located near Kapurthala. Established by an act of Parliament, it caters to students from all over India, including international students from neighbouring countries such as Nepal and Bangladesh. The institute was established in 1960 by then-Prime Minister Jawaharlal Nehru and has played a key role in training Indias most brilliant engineers ever since its founding. It offers courses across seven undergraduate streams.

7) JNTU Hyderabad

Andhra Pradeshs capital city, Visakhapatnam, is home to one of best medical schools in India. The Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU) was established by an act of parliament on December 23, 1989 and has become a key institution for producing medical professionals in South India. JNTU offers both graduate and postgraduate programs through seven colleges, two autonomous institutes and an active research arm. JNTU-Hyderabads College of Medical Sciences graduates over 900 students annually from its highly ranked undergraduate program ranked among top ten public universities in Andhra Pradesh.

8) Jodhpur National University, Jodhpur

Founded in 1966, Jodhpur National University has gained a reputation as one of top universities that offers NEET preparatory courses. The university provides high-quality instruction and is accredited by NABL. Admissions are through entrance exams. In addition to providing NEET tutoring, Jodhpur National University also offers a three-year undergraduate program and a two-year graduate program. Facilities include Wi-Fi access on campus, computers with internet access, digital libraries containing textbooks and ebooks, online virtual classrooms and more. There are state-of-the art labs where students can work on group projects related to their field of study.

9) Manipal Academy of Higher Education, Manipal

Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) has emerged as one of Indias premier institutes for higher education. Located near Udupi, a small coastal town in Karnataka, MAHE is known to provide excellent healthcare facilities and educational infrastructure. The campus boasts of a sophisticated library system with over 120,000 books and a computer lab that supports over 2000 online users. It also has guest houses, hostels and shopping complexes to support students. MAHE offers NEET Exam coaching across all major cities like Bangalore, Chennai, Mumbai, Hyderabad and Kolkata etc., so that students can choose a study center convenient to them. Faculty members are drawn from renowned institutions like Harvard University and IIT Delhi among others.

10) Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University Delhi (GGSIPU Delhi)

A full-fledged residential university with hostels, three middles schools, primary schools and pre-primary schools. The university has library, sports complex and indoor stadiums. It holds educational programmes such as workshops, seminars and symposiums. It imparts training to different hospitals affiliated to it regarding its medical courses. Some of its popular courses are BHMS (Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine & Surgery), BAMS (Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine & Surgery), BCA (Bachelor of Computer Application) and BA (Hons.) Economics to name a few.
